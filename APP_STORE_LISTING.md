### 1.2 Description (5000 characters max)

```
TEPOVKA – Professional Heart Rate & HRV Monitoring for Wellness

Measure your heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), and respiratory 
rate using just your smartphone camera. Tepovka uses advanced photoplethysmography 
(PPG) algorithms to provide real-time cardiovascular insights.

✅ FEATURES:
• Real-time Heart Rate (BPM) measurement
• Heart Rate Variability (HRV) analysis – SDNN, RMSSD, pNN50, SD1, SD2
• Respiratory Rate (RR) tracking
• Continuous HRV metrics (sliding window processing)
• Beautiful chart visualization & history
• Export data as CSV for medical reviews
• HealthKit Integration (iOS 14+)
• Senior-Friendly Interface with Large Text
• Haptic Feedback Support
• No login required – 100% private

🎯 WHAT IT MEASURES:
• BPM (Beats Per Minute) – Your current heart rate
• HRV (Heart Rate Variability) – Measure stress & recovery
• RR (Respiratory Rate) – Breathing frequency
• All metrics calculated locally on your device

⚠️ IMPORTANT – NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE:
Tepovka is a WELLNESS app for general health monitoring, NOT a diagnostic tool.
It should NOT be used as the sole basis for medical decisions.
If you experience chest pain, shortness of breath, or abnormal heart rhythms,
seek professional medical help immediately.

🔐 PRIVACY FIRST:
• NO server uploads – All processing is local to your device
• NO login or account creation required
• NO advertising or tracking
• NO personal data collection beyond your measurements
• Full control of Apple Health sharing

📊 HOW IT WORKS:
Place your finger over the camera and leave it still for 60 seconds.
The app analyzes subtle changes in light reflection to calculate heart metrics.

Designed by VUT Brno for cardiovascular wellness research and education.
```

## 9. Important Notes

✨ **Design Considerations for App Stores:**
- Keep descriptions truthful and not misleading
- No health claims that violate medical device regulations
- Clear limitations on what app can/cannot do
- Easy access to privacy policy & terms

⚠️ **Health App Review Red Flags:**
- Don't claim to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent diseases
- Don't claim clinical accuracy without validation
- Always include medical disclaimers
- Be transparent about limitations

🚀 **Launch Strategy:**
1. Soft launch to selected countries (e.g., Czech Republic first)
2. Monitor reviews and feedback for 2 weeks
3. Expand to EU and English-speaking regions
4. After 1 month of stability, global launch
